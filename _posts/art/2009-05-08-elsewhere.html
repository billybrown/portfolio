Elsewhere is a museum and artist collaborative taking place in a former thrift and surplus store. They invite artsts to come in and create art installations with all of the things in the space.

Jessie and I took part in a month long artist residency at elsewhere where we worked on a number of projects, the main one being a sculpture called Plush The Ride. We collected all of the stuffed animals in the building and created a sort of carnival ride machine for them.
